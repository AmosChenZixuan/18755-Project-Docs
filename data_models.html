get_frequency_ranking() → DataFrame

calculate the frequency ranking for each route in descending order. Route is defined as a pair of origin city and destination city. From city A to city B and from city B to city A are considered the same route here, even though they are counted separately, the maximum will be used as the frequency of the route.

return a pandas.DataFrame with columns [‘origin_city’, ‘destination_city’, ‘count’].
